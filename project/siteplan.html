FitCity Swimming Club Site Plan

Site Name

The site is named FitCity Swimming Club to reflect our commitment to promoting fitness through swimming in our local city community. The name combines "Fit" for health and fitness with "City" to emphasize our location and community focus.

Site Purpose

This website provides comprehensive information about swimming classes, membership plans, coaching staff, training programs, and upcoming events. It serves current and prospective members by offering easy access to schedules, online registration, and contact details to facilitate engagement with the FitCity Swimming Club.

User Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A parent wants to find the most suitable swimming class for their 8-year-old beginner and needs detailed class schedules and age-group information.
  • Scenario 2: An aspiring competitive swimmer looks to browse training programs and register online for specialized coaching sessions.

Color Schema

The following primary colors are used throughout the site:
#00796b (Teal Green): Used for headings, borders, and accent elements to provide a fresh, aquatic feel that represents water and health.
#b2ebf2 (Light Cyan): Applied as background color for list items to maintain a calm, soothing water-inspired palette enhancing readability and user comfort.

Typography

The font family 'Segoe UI' along with fallback fonts Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif is applied globally for balance between modernity and readability. All body text and headings use this font stack to ensure a clean and consistent typographic experience.

Site Sections Overview

  • Home / Landing Page
    Welcome with club highlights and navigation links.
  • Class Schedules
    Weekly swimming classes by skill level and age group.
  • Membership Details
    Plan options, pricing, and exclusive member benefits.
  • Training Programs
    Technique, endurance, and competition swim programs.
  • Coach Profiles
    Meet our certified, passionate coaching team.
  • Upcoming Events
    Gala meets, open houses, workshops, and more.
  • Photo Gallery
    Snapshots & moments from our vibrant swim community.
  • Contact Information
    Email, phone, and club address for inquiries.
  • Online Registration
    Easy sign-up form for new members.

Wireframes

Two wireframe sketches complement this plan:
Mobile wireframe: A simple vertical layout featuring a hamburger menu at top-left, club logo and name centered, a welcoming hero section, followed by clear link buttons for each site section stacked vertically for easy tap navigation.
Desktop wireframe: A wide top navigation bar with horizontally listed menu items under the club logo aligned left, a large hero banner with highlights, and a multi-column layout below showing quick links to schedules, membership, and upcoming events with images or icons.
